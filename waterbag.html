DayOne Purification Waterbag "powered by P&G Packets"

The DayOne Purification Waterbag is a personal water treatment backpack that enables people worldwide to purify dirty water easily in a complete all-in-one device for water collection, transport, treatment, and protected storage. The DayOne WaterbagTM is a 10-liter water backpack designed for use exclusively with the Procter & Gamble water treatment packets that are distributed worldwide.

How to Use the DayOne Purification Waterbag:

Steps shown on universal pictographs:

  1. Fill the Waterbag to the 10-Liter fill-line
  2. Add a P&G Purifier of Water packet
  3. Close the Waterbag
  4. Hang up the bag and rotate to mix for 5 minutes
  5. Settle and disinfect for 25 minutes
  6. Dispense through the integrated filter and drink
